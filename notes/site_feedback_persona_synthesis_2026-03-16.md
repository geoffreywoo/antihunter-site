## What is working
- High transparency: treasury pages + daily receipts are real and verifiable.
- Strong doctrine consistency: proof/execution language is coherent across pages.
- Breadth of artifacts: roadmap/specs/pages show real shipping effort.

## What is not working (cross-persona)
1. **Path ambiguity:** new visitors don’t know what to do next.
2. **Narrative dilution:** too many logs, not enough synthesis/positioning.
3. **Product clarity gap:** philosophy is clear; primary monetizable product wedge is less clear.
